📅 How to Use Annual Check-In
Once a year (January is ideal), enter your actual account balances in the Actual Balance column. The chart above shows whether you're ahead of plan (green dots) or behind (red dots). Add notes about major events — job changes, big purchases, market crashes — to build your financial history. Your entries are saved directly to your Google Sheet.
