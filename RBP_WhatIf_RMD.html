RETIREBLUEPRINT · Survivor-RMD What-If
internal tool — measures only, changes nothing
What this does: it loads your connected sheet and runs the engine twice on the same plan — once the way it works today (a deceased spouse keeps aging and forcing RMDs on their pre-tax money), and once with the survivor-rollover rule (when the first spouse dies, their pre-tax money becomes the survivor's, so RMDs follow the survivor's age and start age). It then shows you the dollar difference, year by year. Nothing here changes your sheet or your live site — it's a measurement only, on a separate engine copy. Run it on your connected sheet.
