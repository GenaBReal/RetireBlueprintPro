Quick Tips
🔄 After saving on Inputs — click Refresh in the nav bar on any page to load your updated numbers immediately. No hard refresh needed.

📊 Social Security — go to ssa.gov/myaccount and use the amount at your Full Retirement Age (FRA), not age 62.

⚠️ IRMAA warning — before a large Roth conversion, check the Tax Engine page first. Too much income in one year triggers Medicare surcharges.

🔄 Roth Ladder — if you plan to retire before 59½, start here. It shows exactly which money you can access and when.

🏠 LTC Planner — even if you're years from needing it, run your numbers once. The reserve amount will surprise you.
If Something Looks Wrong
Check these five things first:
1. Is "Include?" set to Yes in column A for each active account?
2. Are both partner names filled in on the Inputs page?
3. Is the plan start year set to the current year?
4. Click 🔄 Refresh in the nav bar
5. Still stuck? Reply to this email — I respond personally within 24 hours
Updating Your Plan Each Year
Once a year — I suggest your birthday or January 1st — go to the Annual Check-In page and update your account balances, income changes, and any new accounts. Takes about 15 minutes. Your projection updates automatically.
Sharing With Your Financial Advisor
Go to the Summary Report page and click 🖨️ Print / Save PDF. You'll get a clean professional one-pager with your complete plan — income sources, account balances, tax strategy, stress test results, and key milestones.
