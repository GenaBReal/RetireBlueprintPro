Quick Tips
🔄 After saving on Inputs — click Refresh in the nav bar on any page to load your updated numbers immediately. No hard refresh needed.

📊 Social Security — go to ssa.gov/myaccount and use the amount at your Full Retirement Age (FRA), not age 62.

⚠️ IRMAA warning — before a large Roth conversion, check the Tax Engine page first. Too much income in one year triggers Medicare surcharges.

🔄 Roth Ladder — if you plan to retire before 59½, start here. It shows exactly which money you can access and when.
