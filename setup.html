Connect Your Sheet to Your Dashboard
Once your sheet is open, look at the address bar in your browser. Copy the long ID from the URL and paste it below — then click Connect.
How to find your Sheet ID:
Look at the URL in your browser when your sheet is open. It looks like this:
docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoPqRsTuVwXyZ123456789/edit
Copy just the highlighted part — that's your Sheet ID. It's about 44 characters long.
