RETIREBLUEPRINT · Engine Parity Check
internal tool — not customer facing
What this does: it loads your connected Google Sheet, runs the JavaScript engine (rbp-engine.js) on the same inputs, and compares the two projections cell by cell — every year, every column. It tells you the largest dollar gap and exactly where it lives, so you can see whether the JS engine is sound enough to trust for speed. Run it on your own connected sheet (it uses the same saved Sheet ID as your other pages). Your sheet stays the official record; nothing here changes it.
