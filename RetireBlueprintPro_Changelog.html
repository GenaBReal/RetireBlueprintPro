100-year projection engine. Year-by-year portfolio, income, and expense modeling from today through both partners' projected lifespans. Powered by your private Google Sheet. Dashboard
Monte Carlo simulation — 1,000 market scenarios. Runs entirely in your browser using your actual data. Shows success rate, median/worst/best outcomes, fan chart, depletion risk by year, and plain-English verdict with depletion year detail. Stress Test
Stress Test — sequence of returns scenarios. Year-1 market crash (−20% and −30%), high inflation (5%), custom scenario sliders, and base case comparison. Stress Test
Roth Conversion Optimizer. Calculates the ideal annual conversion to minimize lifetime taxes, with IRMAA Medicare surcharge detection and a visual conversion window planner. Tax Engine · Inputs
Roth Access Ladder. Year-by-year visual showing when each account bucket unlocks penalty-free — taxable brokerage, pre-tax 401k, Roth conversions (5-year clock), Roth growth, and HSA. Includes Rule of 55, 72(t) SEPP, and Roth ladder strategy guides. Roth Ladder
Tax Engine with IRMAA modeling. Real 2025 marginal rates, standard deductions, all five IRMAA Medicare surcharge tiers, RMD schedule, and Roth conversion window analysis. Tax Engine
Medicare Planner. All parts of Medicare explained (A, B, C, D, Medigap), IRMAA calculator with your income highlighted, lifetime cost estimate, enrollment timeline, and strategy tips. Medicare
Long-Term Care Planner. National median costs for all 6 care settings, a funded reserve calculator, portfolio impact chart (funded vs. unfunded), and guide to all 6 funding strategies including Medicaid rules. LTC Planner
Healthcare Bridge calculator. Estimates pre-Medicare insurance costs for early retirees — a number most plans miss entirely ($50K–$150K+ for a couple retiring before 65). Inputs
Smile Curve spending model. Three-phase spending (active/settling/healthcare-heavy) with customizable weights — because retirees don't spend a flat amount every year. Dashboard · Inputs
Couples support — fully independent. Two partners with different ages, retirement dates, Social Security, accounts, and RMD calculations. Survivor benefit modeled automatically. All Pages
Social Security optimizer. Both partners' benefits, survivor scenarios, optimal claiming age, COLA adjustment, and break-even analysis. Inputs · Dashboard
FIRE Intelligence panel. FI Date, Safe Withdrawal Rate, FIRE type (Lean/Regular/Fat), and Coast FIRE number — updated automatically as you change your numbers. Dashboard
Annual Check-In system. Guided one-page form to update balances and assumptions each year in about 15 minutes. Keeps your plan accurate forever. Annual Check-In
Print-ready Summary Report. Professional one-pager for sharing with a spouse or financial advisor — all your key numbers, income waterfall, and stress test results. Summary
Setup Guide with full FIRE glossary. Every concept explained from scratch — FIRE, FI, SWR, Coast FIRE, Roth ladder, IRMAA, RMDs, Smile Curve, sequence of returns risk — no financial background required. Setup Guide
Live Demo mode. Full tool with sample data — try every page before connecting your sheet. Demo
