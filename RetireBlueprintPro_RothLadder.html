📅 Roth Conversion Window Planner
The Roth Conversion Window is the period between your retirement date and age 73 (when RMDs begin). During this window your taxable income is lower — making conversions cheaper. Each conversion starts its own 5-year clock before the principal can be withdrawn penalty-free.
📖 Withdrawal Order Strategy
The right withdrawal order can save tens of thousands in taxes and extend your portfolio by years. The general rule: draw from accounts in the order that keeps your overall tax bill lowest — but it depends on your specific situation.
