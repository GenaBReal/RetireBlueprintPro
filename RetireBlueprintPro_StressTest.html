💡 How to Improve Your Resilience
📖 What is Sequence of Returns Risk? The biggest threat to early retirees isn't a bad market — it's a bad market at the WRONG TIME. Retiring into a crash means you're selling assets at low prices to fund living expenses. Those sold shares never recover for you. A portfolio losing 30% in year 1 while withdrawing 5%/yr can run out decades earlier than a portfolio that has the same crash in year 20. Your plan's Roth allocation, SS income floor, and spending flexibility are your three main defenses.
