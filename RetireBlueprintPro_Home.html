Why this exists: I set out to find a spreadsheet on Etsy that would meet our DIY retirement planning needs — I bought several but none covered what I was really looking for, so I decided to make my own … and well, I may have gone a bit further than JUST a spreadsheet — I wanted it to look nice (I'm a graphic designer, so I couldn't help myself) and also have all the information we might need throughout our pre-planning and retirement years. This is all mine — for my family, but I think you will find it WAY more comprehensive than what is out there for us DIYers. And the math? Rock solid — every tax, Medicare, and Social Security figure comes straight from the official sources (the IRS, CMS, and SSA) and checks out to the dollar against your own sheet. Best of all, it's just a one-time fee — NOT a subscription. I hate those! Hope you find it as useful as we do! Enjoy! —Gena
