What is FIRE? — Financial Independence, Retire Early
FIRE — Financial Independence, Retire Early — comes down to one idea: save steadily, invest sensibly, and retire on a timeline you choose. RetireBlueprint Pro gives you the numbers behind it: your FI Date (when work becomes optional), your Safe Withdrawal Rate (whether you're drawing too much), your Coast FIRE number (when you can stop saving), and your FIRE type — Lean, Regular, or Fat — based on how you plan to spend. You don't have to be 30 or chasing an extreme budget to use it. This is for anyone who wants more control over when and how they retire.
